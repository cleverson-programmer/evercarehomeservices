Common Questions

Questions Before Reaching Out?

We've answered the most common questions below — if yours isn't here, just text us.

What are your business hours?

We're available Monday through Saturday, 8am–7pm. Sunday we're closed, but texts sent over the weekend are always answered first thing Monday morning. You can text us anytime — we never leave a message without a reply.

Text is by far the fastest. Send us a message at (508) 736-1263 and we typically respond within 1–2 hours during business hours. It's casual, easy, and you'll speak directly with our team.

Do you offer both care and cleaning services?

Yes — EverCare Home Services offers both certified in-home care for seniors and professional residential and commercial cleaning. Many families find it convenient to work with one trusted provider for both needs.

Is there any obligation when I reach out?

None at all. Every conversation starts with a free, no-pressure consultation. We'll listen to your needs and help you understand your options — no commitment, no forms, no rush.

How quickly can services begin?

In most cases we can schedule your first visit within a few days of your initial contact. We understand that families sometimes need support quickly and we always do our best to accommodate.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Worcester and surrounding communities in Central Massachusetts including Shrewsbury, Auburn, Holden, Leicester, Millbury, Grafton, Northborough, Southborough, Spencer, West Boylston, and Marlborough. Not sure if your area is covered? Just ask — we may still be able to help.

How is pricing determined?

Every home and every care situation is different — so pricing is always based on the specific service, property size, and frequency needed. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all rates. After a brief conversation about your needs, we'll give you a clear, honest number with no hidden costs and no obligation to commit.
