Common Questions

Common Questions About Our Cleaning Services

Honest answers to the questions clients ask us most often.

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?

Yes. Our team arrives fully equipped with professional-grade cleaning products and all the tools needed to do the job right. You don't need to provide anything — just let us in.

Who will be cleaning my home or business?

With EverCare, you know exactly who is coming. We introduce our team before the first visit and work hard to maintain consistency. You won't be dealing with different people every time — you build a relationship with a dedicated, trained team.

Are your cleaners background checked and insured?

Yes. Every team member is fully background-checked before entering any client property. We are also fully licensed and insured — so you're always protected.

How quickly can cleaning begin after the first contact?

In many cases, we can schedule your first clean within a few days of your initial conversation. We understand that some clients need help quickly, and we do our best to accommodate.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel?

Life happens — we get it. Just let us know by text as soon as possible and we'll work with you to find a new time. We keep things flexible and don't make rescheduling difficult.

How do I get a cleaning estimate?

The easiest way is to send us a text at (508) 736-1263. Tell us about your space and what you're looking for, and we'll respond quickly with an honest estimate — no pressure, no commitment required.
