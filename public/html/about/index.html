Who We Are

A Family Business Built on Care

EverCare Home Services was founded by Renan and Lalesca — a couple based in Worcester, Massachusetts who decided to build something together: a business that treats every client like a neighbor, not a contract.

We started with a simple belief — that people deserve to feel safe and supported in their own homes. Whether that means a spotless kitchen after a long week, or a compassionate caregiver by their side, EverCare is here to make it happen.

Every client gets our full attention. You're not a ticket in a queue. You're a family we've committed to — and that standard applies to every professional on our team.

What We Believe

The Values That Drive Us

These aren't slogans on a wall. They're the standard behind every visit — and what every client can count on from EverCare.

Trustworthiness

We enter people's homes — that's a privilege we take seriously. Every member of our team is background-checked, licensed, and accountable.

Genuine Compassion

We don't just clean homes or assist seniors — we care about the people living in them. Every interaction is personal, not transactional.

Consistent Quality

You should get the same excellent service on the 10th visit as on the first. We don't let standards slip over time.

Clear Communication

We respond fast, explain everything clearly, and never leave you wondering. Questions are always welcome — we prefer honesty over silence.

Community First

We're not a national franchise. We're Worcester locals, and we invest in the well-being of the community we call home.

Accountability

If something isn't right, we make it right. No excuses, no runaround. You deserve a company that stands behind its work.
